Please take the time to read our rules.
You are responsible for knowing the rules.
--------General rules--------
  1. Any form of hacking or scripting (Avoiding AFK Timer, Aimbot,...) will lead to permanent ban.
  2. Do not spam (this includes chat, camera, stunstick, microphone / voice,...)
  3. In game currency may not be sold or traded for real life items, currency or credits.
  4. A staff member has final say on any in-game disputes.
  5. No promoting other servers.
  6. No prop killing
  7. No randomly killing (RDM)
  8. No moderator or admin may use noclip unless they are using the "Staff on duty" job
  9. Do not raid if someone has a "building sign"
  10. Wait at least 20 minutes before raiding somebody for a second time
  11. wait at least 15 minutes before mugging somebody for a second time
--------Job rules--------
  1. Police may arrest or kill hitmen after the kill of a target.
  2. Police must attempt to arrest before killing, and must only kill if their life or others is in danger
  3. Gun dealer must not self supply, except to defend his own base.
  4. Medic may be hired to heal or pocket heal, but can not partake in raids.
  5. Guard may guard bases that are printing, but can not partake in printing.
  6. Parkourist can not raid or print.
  7. Police may not own bases or doors. But the mayor can build inside the PD
  8. Any government job must not own printers.
  9. The mayor must state a reason for a lockdown if he makes a lockdown.
  10. The mayor can own any non-illegal weapons.
  11. You may shoot hobos if they are throwing bugbait after warning them to stop.
  12. Hobos may build bases on the sidewalk, just try not to over do it.
--------Base rules--------
  1. You may use one-way props, but you must walk through them to shoot.
  2. 3 max fading doors, 3 max printer fading doors per base.
  3. Crouch spaces are not allowed.
  4. All fading doors must have accessible keypads.
  5. Keypads must hold open for at least 5 seconds.
  6. Mazes are allowed, just don't make them too long or make them invisible/black.
  7. You are allowed to KoS somebody if they are inside your security system/base, but if you want to KoS people on your property put a KoS line first
  8. You can not hide (even partially) inside a no collided prop.
  9. Do not unfade a prop and then shoot through it.
